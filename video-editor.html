Video Editor

Job Description

We are looking for a skilled Video Editor to create engaging content for our audience. You will work with our creative team to produce high-quality videos for marketing, social media, and product demonstrations. The ideal candidate has a strong sense of storytelling and visual aesthetics.

Skills Required

  • Proven experience as a Video Editor
  • Proficiency in video editing software (Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro)
  • Knowledge of motion graphics and visual effects (After Effects)
  • Understanding of storytelling and pacing
  • Experience with color correction and audio editing
  • Ability to work with various video formats and resolutions
  • Creative mindset and attention to detail

Key Responsibilities

  • Edit and assemble raw footage into engaging finished videos
  • Create motion graphics and visual effects for videos
  • Add sound effects, music tracks, and voice-overs
  • Color grade and enhance visual quality
  • Optimize videos for different platforms and formats
  • Collaborate with the creative team to develop compelling content
  • Stay updated with new editing techniques and trends
