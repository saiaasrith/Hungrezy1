Growth Lead

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Growth Lead to drive our business expansion strategies. In this role, you will be responsible for identifying growth opportunities, implementing marketing initiatives, and optimizing conversion funnels. You will work closely with our product, marketing, and analytics teams to achieve sustainable growth.

Skills Required

  • Proven experience in growth marketing or related field
  • Strong analytical skills and data-driven approach
  • Experience with A/B testing and conversion rate optimization
  • Knowledge of digital marketing channels (SEO, SEM, social media)
  • Familiarity with analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)
  • Understanding of product development processes
  • Excellent project management and leadership skills

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement growth strategies
  • Identify key performance indicators and track metrics
  • Design and run A/B tests to optimize conversion rates
  • Analyze user behavior and recommend product improvements
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to execute growth initiatives
  • Manage acquisition channels and optimize CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
  • Stay updated with industry trends and competitive landscape
